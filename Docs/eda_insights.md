# EDA Insight Memo — Data Analyst/Scientist Job Market
**Data source:** LinkedIn Data Jobs Dataset (Apr–Jun 2025), 453 cleaned postings
**Date analyzed:** [today's date]

## Key Findings

1. **Role split:** Data Analyst roles slightly outnumber Data Scientist roles 
   (267 vs 186, ~59% vs 41%).

2. **Employer concentration:** Meta alone accounts for ~7% of all postings (30/453), 
   the single largest employer by a wide margin — followed by Netflix, AARATECH, 
   FanDuel, and Google.

3. **Geographic spread:** Postings are concentrated in major US tech hubs 
   (New York, San Francisco), with meaningful international representation 
   in London and Bengaluru, India.

## Limitations

- ~11% of postings list only "United States" with no specific city, limiting 
  granular geographic analysis for that subset.
- Meta's outsized share of postings (~7%) may skew overall skill-demand findings 
  toward large-tech-company requirements rather than the broader market average.
- Dataset reflects a 3-month snapshot (Apr–Jun 2025); trends over longer time 
  periods cannot be assessed.

Proceed to NLP-based skill extraction from job descriptions to identify the 
most in-demand skills for each role category.
